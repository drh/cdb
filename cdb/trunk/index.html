cdb, A Machine-Independent Debugger

cdb is a machine-independent debugger for C programs compiled by lcc 4.x, a retargetable compiler for ISO Standard C. cdb is described in the paper

D. R. Hanson and M. Raghavachari,
A Machine-Independent Debugger,
Software—Practice and Experience 26 (11), 1277-1299, Nov. 1996.
